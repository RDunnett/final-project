About Us

Our History

J.M. Automotive began its journey in 2010 when Jim McKenny laid the foundation in Kemptville. What started as a vision to offer exceptional automotive care blossomed into a thriving family-owned establishment. Over the years, our commitment to quality service and dedication to our rural community have been the pillars of our growth. Today, J.M. Automotive stands tall, a testament to our founder's passion and our team's unwavering dedication to excellence in auto repair and customer satisfaction.

Inclusivity

We are committed to inclusivity so that we can to positive social change. By being an ally and standing against discrimination, we become part of a movement towards equality and justice. Land acknowledgement

We acknowledge that we work and live on aboriginal land that has been inhabited by Indigenous peoples from the beginning. As settlers, we're grateful for the opportunity to be here and we thank all the generations of people who have taken care of this land - for thousands of years. Long before today, as we work here, there have been aboriginal peoples who have been the stewards of this place In particular, we acknowledge the traditional territory of the Anishnabek, Huron-Wendat, Haudenosaunee (Iroquois), Oneida and Haudenosaunee (St Lawrence Iroquois) peoples. This territory is covered by the Upper Canada Treaties. We recognize and deeply appreciate their historic connection to this place. We also recognize the contributions of MÃ©tis, Inuit, and other Indigenous peoples have made, both in shaping and strengthening this community in particular, and our province and country as a whole. As settlers, this recognition of the contributions and historic importance of Indigenous peoples must also be clearly and overtly connected to our collective commitment to make the promise and the challenge of Truth and Reconciliation real in our communities, and in particular to bring justice for murdered and missing indigenous women and girls across our country.

Meet The Team!

Meet the exceptional individuals behind J.M. Automotive's success. Our team is a close-knit group of automotive enthusiasts, led by seasoned professionals with a shared passion for cars and a commitment to stellar service. Each member brings a unique set of skills and expertise, united by a dedication to providing top-notch care for your vehicle. Get acquainted with the friendly faces ready to ensure your automotive needs are met with precision and a smile.

Jim McKenney

J.M. Automotive opened in 2010. The owner, Jim McKenney, went to Kemptville College for Diesel Mechanics in 1994, and started his apprenticeship at Christie Chrysler, in Kemptville. He earned his license in 1999. When Christie Chrysler was bought out by Car Canada, Jim moved with them to Ottawa, where he stayed until 2010. Jim was born and raised in Oxford Station, and he resides there still with his wife (see below) and 2 kids. Jim works on every make and model, from 1914 Model T, to 2023 Dodge Rams. Jim is also a professor at Algonquin College, where he teaches the up and coming mechanics. Jim has worked hard to build up his reputation and client base. We are happy to report that we have many happy customers, some who have followed Jim from shop to shop.
