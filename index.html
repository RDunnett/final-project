Welcome!

Welcome to J.M. Automotive, where family values meet automotive expertise and where you get honest work, for a fair price. For over a decade, we have proudly served our community, offering comprehensive auto repair services for all vehicle makes and models.

At J.M. Automotive, our dedicated team of licensed technicians combines personalized service with cutting-edge technology to ensure your vehicle runs at its best. Through strong partnerships with trusted suppliers, we secure quality parts at competitive prices, backed by a solid 1-year parts and labor warranty. Plus, our exclusive program provides roadside assistance with the purchase of AC Delco parts, enhancing your peace of mind on the road.
